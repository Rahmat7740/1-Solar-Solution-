FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about going solar? We have answers to the most common questions our customers ask.

How much does a solar system cost in Attock?▼
A basic 5KW system starts from around Rs. 700,000–900,000 including installation. Cost depends on system size, type (on-grid, off-grid, hybrid), and battery storage. We provide free site assessments and detailed quotes with no obligation.
How long does installation take?▼
For a standard residential system, installation is typically completed within 48 hours after permits are approved. Larger commercial or industrial projects may take 3–7 days depending on scope.
What is net metering and how does it work?▼
Net metering lets you sell excess solar electricity back to WAPDA/DISCO. Your meter runs backwards when production exceeds usage, earning bill credits. 1° Solar Solution handles the entire net metering application process for you.
Do I need a lithium battery with solar panels?▼
Not always. On-grid systems work without batteries. However, given Pakistan's load-shedding, adding a lithium battery storage system ensures 24/7 power regardless of grid outages — highly recommended for Attock.
What warranties do you provide?▼
25-year performance warranty on panels, 10-year product warranty on inverters, 10-year warranty on LiFePO4 batteries, and a 10-year workmanship guarantee on all our installations.
Do you serve areas outside Attock?▼
Yes! While based in Attock, we serve all of Punjab including Rawalpindi, Chakwal, Mianwali, Jhelum, and surrounding districts. Contact us to discuss your project location.
